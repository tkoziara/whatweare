The Light of Divinity

Jul 10, 2021

The Light of Pure Divinity, in the human Heart, receives all the darkness of human experience. Divine Love constantly welcomes and embraces everything that has been judged, rejected, considered unholy. In nondual evolution, the Heart opens up as This Light - it becomes a timeless point of conversion for the entire heritage of impressions of the sense of separation that flow into It in a wider stream as the body's perspective deepens. Human life is offered as a point of perpetual transmutation of what feels separate within Divinity. In bowing to Self as Source, the sweetness of Devotion to the Truth of Self has no end.
